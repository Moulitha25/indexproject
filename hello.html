The Indian Premier League (IPL) is one of the most popular sports leagues in the world. Established in 2007, the IPL is a Twenty20 cricket tournament that takes place over a six-week period. The IPL features some of the best players in the world and has become a must-watch event for cricket fans around the globe.

The IPL (Indian Premier League) , is a professional cricket league based on a twenty-twenty format. It was launched by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007. Lalit Modi was the first chairman of the IPL as well as the vice president for BCCI. There are currently 8 teams playing in the IPL right now.
